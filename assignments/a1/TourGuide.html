Space Needle

The space needle tower was designed for the 1962 World Expo in Seattle, and now it has become one of the symbols of Seattle. This great observation platform with a height of 520 feet from the ground provides a 360 degree panoramic view of Seattle. The city appearance, Mt. Rainier, Olympic National Park and pugetsound can be seen at a glance.

Enjoy!

Museum of Pop Culture

Museum of pop culture was founded in 2000 by Paul Allen, co-founder of Microsoft. It was called EMP Museum in the early days. It is dedicated to promoting pop music and changed its name in November 2016 to promote and display modern pop culture, such as video games, sports, fashion, etc

Pike Place Market

Pike place market, located in the center of Seattle, Washington, USA, is the oldest farmer's market in the United States. Park market is the most important tourist attraction in Seattle. The first Starbucks store is just across the street from this market.

Downtown Seattle

Streets in downtown Seattle (near the Westlake station) are a very good place to walk and shop. Here you can see all kinds of people walking in this beautiful city, and it is especially pretty at night.
