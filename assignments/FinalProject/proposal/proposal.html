Overview

Scope: This site will be the mobile version of my 426 final project. In this platform, users can request a project by choosing the categories on the nav bar, and selecting their service types. They will be entering the start and due dates of their project, and they will be able to view their requested projects on "My project" nav tab.

Problem: This mobile site will solve the user experience problem of the desktop version. When users request their project on the phone, they will be able to have a user-friendly mobile version of the project requestor.

Intended Audience

My target group is people who are looking for service in web development, web design, and web management. For this project, it is for poeple who are requesting service on their phone.

I believe this platform will help them to request projects from me easily, and I will try my best to make this site accessible to operate to ensure it is for people with all levels of computer literacy.
